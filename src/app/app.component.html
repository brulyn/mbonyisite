About us

As part of the drive to spread the gospel, to raise worshippers and reach encourage Christians in their walk with Christ, Israel Eric Mbonyicyambu commonly known as Mbonyi was inspired by God to establish the 12 Stones Ministry. A gospel music ministry in Rwanda

About us

What we do

It's not about fame, fortune or even playing simply out of the love of the music ... it's a mission and a ministry. It's the Great Commission, put to a tune and sharing God's glory with those who have never seen it, those who just need to be reminded of it or those who are searching for it.

Vision & Mission

Our first responsibility is to reach the area where God placed us. We need to get the Gospel to EVERYONE within reach (Mark 16:15). Reaching the lost is every Christian’s business. But as of today it remains unfinished.

VisionMission

Experiencing God

Experiencing God is not just our commitment to know about God, but to know him intimately, encounter him through worship, prayer, salvation and the present manifestation of the Holy Spirit in the lives of all nations and generations.

Live a Godly Lifestyle

Our love for God should be demonstrated in our lifestyle. This is through the fruit of the Spirit love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, and self-control, and to forsake all sin by saying “NO” to ungodliness through the amazing grace given to us.

Extend the Kingdom

We are committed to spreading the gospel of the Kingdom by extending the invitation to all mankind to come and experience God’s forgiveness and mercy through Jesus Christ and the power of the Holy Spirit.

Excellence

We believe that a God as great as ours deserves no less than the best we have to give. Through our music, we want to do all that we can for His glory. Therefore, in all we do, we are always going to try to make it better, not for fame or pride, but for the joy of hearing His “Well Done good and faithful servant!” “And whatever you do, in word or in deed, do it all in the name of the Lord Jesus” Colossians 3:17 How excellent is His name.
